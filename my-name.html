Some notes regarding my name

First things first, my first name is Mei Rose, not Mei. I therefore have no middle name.

The pronunciation of my name is the following: [ˈmeɪ ɹoʊz ˈkanɚ]

You may hear some people call me Mei. The approximately accurate guideline for this is as follows: If you would address me using the informal form of the word you (examples include German du, French tu, and Dutch jij), you may call me Mei

In the case that you would address me using the formal form of the word you (examples include German Sie, French vous, and Dutch u), I would appreciate it if you would call me Mei Rose.
